Description

The Russell Hobbs electronic microwave offers programmable, multi-stage cooking with defrost options by time or weight. Featuring quick start and kitchen timer functions, it is designed with an enamel interior for easy cleaning and provides reliable performance with 11 power levels.

Features:

  • 11 Power levels.
  • Smart Info function.
  • Defrost by time or weight.
  • Elegant mirror finish front.
  • User-friendly control panel.
  • Easy to use push button door.
  • Child lock safety feature.
  • Digital LED display with clock.
  • 99-minute cooking timer with end signal.
  • 8 Auto menus: cook, defrost, popcorn, potato, frozen vegetable, beverage, dinner plate, and pizza.

Specifications:

  • Weight: 11.4kg.
  • Assembled Dimensions: 44 x 30 x 24cm.
  • Internal Dimensions: 26 x 16 x 24cm.
  • Material: Stainless Steel, Enamel & Glass.
  • Colour: Silver.
  • Capacity: 20 Litre.
  • Power: 700W.
  • Plate Diameter: 23cm.
  • Warranty: 24 Months (12 months Retail + 12 months extended upon registration at www.russellhobbs.co.za).
